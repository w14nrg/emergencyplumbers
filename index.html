About Emergency Plumbers London

Night-Only Emergency Plumber within 10km of W14 9BP

Emergency Plumbers London is a dedicated night-time emergency plumbing service based in West Kensington (W14 9BP). We operate every night from 9pm to 6am, focusing solely on genuine emergencies where water is escaping, property is at risk or essential services have failed.

We cover approximately a 10km radius from W14 9BP, including West Kensington, Kensington, Chelsea, Fulham, Hammersmith, Shepherd’s Bush and nearby areas. Our aim is to attend quickly, stop the immediate problem, make the property safe and prevent further damage. Follow-up work, full bathroom installs and other planned plumbing can then be booked separately on our daytime number.

  • Burst and leaking pipes
  • Leaking isolation valves and flexi hoses
  • Blocked or overflowing toilets and waste pipes
  • Overflowing tanks, cisterns and ball valves sticking open
  • Emergency make-safe of pipework after accidental damage
  • Noisy, leaking or overflowing cold water storage

Night emergencies only: Please do not use the emergency mobile to book general daytime plumbing. For bathroom installations, upgrades and non-urgent work, call our daytime office number below.
